The Digital Humanities Summer Institute Nepal is a programme of lectures and workshops on Digital Humanities (DH) theory and methods designed to raise awareness of and cultivate experience with this growing field to Nepal. The programme, sponsored by the Institute of Advanced Communication, Education and Research (IACER) with the aid of USEF Nepal. Participants will gain experience with common scholarly questions and tools in the Digital Humanities and will consider how DH research and pedagogy can develop in Nepal with a local accent.
